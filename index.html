1+1English!

Language Learning

Our website focuses on teaching non-English speaking students English, but for an academic setting. Students can complete their coursework without falling behind due a language barrier.

Choose Your Courses

Any user can select the courses they want to study English in. With a wide variety of courses at K-12 levels (including AP courses), student are guaranteed to be able to study English in a course they are taking at their school.

For Schools

1+1 English! has options for schools, including a grading system, class overviews, and priority support. For more information on 1+1English! for Education, conctact us.
